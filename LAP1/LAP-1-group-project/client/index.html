Singapore, Michigan

There's a ghost town, Singapore, buried under the Saugatuck sand dunes in Michigan. After the Great Michigan Fire, much of the trees protecting the city from the harsh winds of Lake Michigan were cut down. The combination of winds and sand eroded the buildings, until they were buried.
